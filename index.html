Guess The Number is a very simple game, just pick any number between the amounts displayed on the screen. Your goal is to find out the secret number before your opponent does so. You'll take turns in the guessing, just type your guessed number on the input and hit enter or click/touch the button Guess.

Enjoy your guessing and good luck!

This game was designed, illustrated and developed by Victor Freire Oliveira, through VFO Studio, as a personal project in Vanilla JavaScript, just for fun and practice.


You can find out more in my GitHub and VFO Studio main page.


The sotware and OS used were all free. Some examples include: VS Code Studio, Gimp, Inkscape and Krita, running on a Linux Ubuntu OS.
